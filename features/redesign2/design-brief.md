# Redesign 2 - Design Brief

## Vision Statement

Transform the portfolio from a dark, gothic aesthetic to a lighter, more experimental space while maintaining the bold, unapologetic creative vision. The off-white background (#C9C8C7) creates a new canvas that allows videos and content to stand out differently, while the "LOST FILES" page introduces a striking, glitchy statement piece.

## Design Principles

### 1. Inverted Contrast
- **Background**: Off-white (#C9C8C7) instead of black
- **Text**: Dark (black/dark gray) instead of white
- **Videos**: Still the main event, but now on light canvas
- **Contrast**: High contrast maintained through dark elements on light background

### 2. Work Hub Structure
- **Work Page**: Central hub with clear navigation
- **Videos/Photos Split**: Separate pages for better organization
- **Arrow Navigation**: Visual indicator for sub-pages
- **Choice Interface**: When clicking Work, user chooses Videos or Photos

### 3. "LOST FILES" Statement
- **Hero Section**: Massive, bold, glitchy text
- **Weirdcore Aesthetic**: Intentional glitches, digital artifacts
- **Striking Impact**: Unforgettable first impression
- **Experimental**: Breaks conventions, makes statement

### 4. Maintained Elements
- **Navigation**: Keep MinimalNav styling exactly as is
- **Video-First**: Videos still dominate (80% visual weight)
- **Experimental Typography**: Carson's rule-breaking approach
- **Glitch Effects**: Weirdcore's intentional digital artifacts

## Aesthetic Direction

### Color Psychology
- **Off-White Background**: Creates space, feels more open, less oppressive
- **Dark Text**: Maintains readability, creates strong contrast
- **Accent Colors**: Red/green/pink still used for gore core, brat green, indie sleaze
- **Inversion**: Dark videos on light background = different visual impact

### Typography Approach
- **Bold Headlines**: Still oversized, experimental
- **Dark Text**: Black/dark gray on light background
- **Glitch Text**: Enhanced for "LOST FILES" hero
- **Experimental Layouts**: Asymmetrical, rotated, overlapping

## Target Experience

1. **Land on Home**: See video grid immediately on light background
2. **Navigate to Work**: See hub with Videos/Photos options
3. **Choose Content**: Click Videos or Photos, see respective portfolio
4. **Discover "Other"**: Encounter striking "LOST FILES" glitchy hero
5. **Contact**: Dark aesthetic with grain texture, experimental form layout, glitch effects on focus

## Success Metrics

- **Visual Impact**: "LOST FILES" page is unforgettable
- **Navigation Clarity**: Work hub is intuitive
- **Contrast**: Dark elements pop on light background
- **Cohesion**: All pages feel part of same system
- **Boldness**: Still experimental, still memorable
